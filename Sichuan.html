Sichuan province is the origin of many dishes that are well-received globally. Originated from the Southwestern China, the food is the most widely served cuisine in China itself. Sichuan cuisine are known for their deep and rich flavors, especially the taste of Sichuan pepper which is rarely found in any other regional cuisines.
