Cantonese cuisine originates from SE China around Hong Kong, and it is one of the most widely served style of Chinese cuisine in the world. This is because most Chinese immigrants who set up restaurants overseas were from Guangdong.

What distinguishes Cantonese food is lightly cooked vegetables and meat, and sweet sauces.
